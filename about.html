100m is a student-run studio collective at OCAD that aims to support co-operation within our community. 100m is an ever-growing network of self-governing studios that aim to create a space fostering interconnection among students from various disciplines to publish, share, create and cultivate a communal practice —to bring to life projects we deeply care about.
