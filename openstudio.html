Open studio!
Open studio is 100m's weekly meeting event, open for everyone to join. We will organize screenings, workshops, and collaborative-making events. Open studio is a chance for us to meet peers, discuss projects, and design for our community.
You have an idea for a workshop? a lecture? a screening? open studio is all yours, just send us an email to plan and organize your event.
